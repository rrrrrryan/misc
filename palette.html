The Claude Code palette

Every color token behind the session browser, glossary, and Cursor viewer — warm cream surfaces, a terracotta clay accent, and one little crab. Each has a light and a dark hex; all are CSS custom properties like var(--accent).

👆 Click a swatch to copy its var(), or a hex chip to copy the value. The chip matching the current theme is highlighted — toggle the theme, top-right, to watch the whole palette shift.
Palette source of truth: ~/code/claude-sessions/assets_data.py (light :root + dark _DARK_VARS). Companion to the design glossary. 🎨
